About us

Call of Duty, launched in 2003 by Infinity Ward and published by Activision, began as a WWII shooter. It quickly evolved, introducing modern and futuristic warfare, and became renowned for its cinematic single-player campaigns and competitive multiplayer modes. The series has expanded to include numerous sequels, spin-offs, and mobile games. It remains one of the best-selling video game franchises of all time, constantly innovating and setting industry standards
